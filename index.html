ABOUT ME



Hello, my name is Ashfaq Maqbool, and I am a CAD/CAM designer. I have a passion for using technology and software to bring my designs to life, and I take pride in creating innovative and efficient products and components for various industries.

SERVICES


As a CAD/CAM design specialist, the following services can be offered :


1. 3D Modeling and Design: Creation of detailed 3D models of products or components, including product design, engineering drawings, and prototypes.
2. Parametric Modeling: Creation of flexible, parametric models that can be easily modified to incorporate design changes.
3. Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM): Generating machine-readable instructions for CNC machines and other automated machinery, including setting up and running simulations to optimize toolpaths and cutting parameters.
4. CNC Programming: Writing the code that drives CNC machines, including specifying the toolpath, feed rate, and other key parameters required to produce the part.
5. Toolpath Generation: Generating the paths that cutting tools will follow on a part or workpiece, taking into account material removal, cutting speed, and tool engagement.
